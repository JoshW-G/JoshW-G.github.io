About Me

My Name is Josh Gardner, I'm currently studying my Master at the University of Birmingha in Advanced Computer Science, having graduated from Bangor University with a 2:1 BSc in Computer Science. I have expertise in C/C++, Java, Python, SQL/MySQL/SQLite, Web Development and am capable of Full Stack Development as well as Git version control. I have also worked with CNN's and object detection, which is the basis of my dissertation and published research project which I presented at Computer Graphics & Visual Computing (CGVC) 2020.
