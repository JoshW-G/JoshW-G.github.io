About Me

I am currently studying my MSc in Advanced Computer Science at the University of Birmingham, after completing my undergrad in CompSci at Bangor University. That is where the work I did for my dissertation, specific food detection in MRI scans of the stomach, was further used in a larger research project involving INRA-AgroParisTech and CNRS which was published and presented by me at Eurographic's CGVC 2020.
I have experience in many different programming languages, and many different libraies and frameworks from more common languages such as C++, Java, Python to OpenCV, MATLAB, Scikit-Learn and Tensorflow. Using these skills I've developed solutions utilising UI, databases, machine learning, deep learning, image processing.
